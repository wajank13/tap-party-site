1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Janko Tech Ventures LLC, operating under the brand Tap Party ("we," "our," or "us"), collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use the Tap Party mobile application (the "App") and our website at tap-party.com (the "Site"), collectively the "Services."

By using the Services, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Services.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Player names: When you start a game, you may enter player names. These names exist only in your device's memory for the duration of the session and are never transmitted to our servers or any third party. They are cleared when the app is closed or the session ends.

Support requests: If you contact us for help, you may provide your name, email address, and a description of your issue. We use this only to respond to your request.

Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we and our third-party partners may automatically collect:

  • Device identifiers — such as your advertising ID (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android) and device model
  • Operating system and app version
  • Usage data — screens viewed, features used, session duration, and crash reports
  • IP address — used to infer general geographic region (country/state level)
  • In-app events — such as game starts, challenge completions, and purchase actions

This data is collected through Firebase Analytics and Google AdMob, described in the Third-Party Services section.

Purchase Information

If you make a purchase (Premium subscription, Lifetime, or Party Pack), the transaction is processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment card details. RevenueCat, our in-app purchase platform, receives purchase receipts and device identifiers to verify and manage your entitlements.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the App and its features
  • Verify and manage premium subscriptions and purchases
  • Serve and personalize advertisements (free tier)
  • Analyze app performance, fix bugs, and improve the user experience
  • Understand which features are most used and prioritize development
  • Respond to your support inquiries
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for purposes incompatible with those listed above without first updating this policy and, where required, obtaining your consent.

4. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party SDKs and services. Each operates under its own privacy policy; we encourage you to review them.

RevenueCat

Purpose: In-app purchase management and subscription entitlements.

Data collected: Purchase receipts, device identifiers, app version, and platform.

Privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

Google AdMob

Purpose: Serving advertisements to free-tier users.

Data collected: Advertising ID (IDFA / GAID), device information, IP address, general location, and ad interaction data. AdMob may use this data to serve personalized ads based on your interests.

Opt-out: You can limit ad tracking in your device settings: iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking; Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID.

Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Firebase (Google)

Purpose: Analytics, crash reporting, and app performance monitoring.

Data collected: Device identifiers, app events, session data, crash logs, and operating system information. Data is aggregated and used to improve the App.

Opt-out: You can disable analytics data collection within the App's Settings screen.

Privacy policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Apple App Store and Google Play also collect data about your device and transactions in accordance with their own privacy policies when you download or purchase through those platforms.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: With RevenueCat, Google AdMob, and Firebase as described above, solely to operate the Services.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or government authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Tap Party, our users, or others.
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred. We will notify you via the App or this page before such a transfer occurs.
  • With your consent: For any other purpose, with your explicit consent.

6. Advertising & Tracking

Free-tier users see advertisements served by Google AdMob. These ads may be personalized based on your device's advertising identifier and general interests. Premium users (any paid plan) do not see ads.

App Tracking Transparency (iOS 14+): On iOS, we request permission before accessing your IDFA for personalized advertising. If you decline, you may still see ads but they will not be personalized.

Google's ad personalization: You can review and adjust Google's ad personalization settings at adssettings.google.com.

7. Data Retention

Player names: Not retained. Cleared from memory at session end.

Purchase records: RevenueCat retains purchase history as long as your account is active or as required to resolve disputes and comply with legal obligations.

Analytics data: Firebase retains event data for up to 14 months by default, after which it is automatically deleted or anonymized.

Support correspondence: Retained for up to 2 years after your last contact, then deleted.

To request deletion of any data associated with you, contact us at support@tap-party.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Tap Party is an adult drinking game intended exclusively for users who are of legal drinking age in their jurisdiction (21+ in the United States; 18+ in most other countries). The App displays an age gate at launch requiring confirmation of legal drinking age.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If we become aware that a person under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete it promptly. If you believe a minor has used the App, please contact us at support@tap-party.com.

This App is not directed at children and is not designed to appeal to children. It is not compliant with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) or similar children's privacy frameworks, and its use by minors is strictly prohibited.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out of personalized ads: Adjust tracking via your device settings (see Section 6).
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time without affecting prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@tap-party.com. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

10. California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) grants you additional rights:

  • Right to Know: The categories and specific pieces of personal information we collect, use, and share.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information, with limited exceptions.
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. No opt-out is currently required, but you may contact us to confirm.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

To submit a CCPA request, email support@tap-party.com with "CCPA Request" in the subject line. We will verify your identity and respond within 45 days (extendable by an additional 45 days with notice).

Categories of personal information collected in the past 12 months: Identifiers (device IDs, IP address); Commercial information (purchase records); Internet/network activity (app usage, crash data). We do not collect sensitive personal information as defined by the CPRA.

11. International Users

Tap Party is operated from the United States. If you access the Services from outside the US, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate.

European Economic Area, United Kingdom, and Switzerland: If you are located in these regions, the legal basis for processing your data is:

  • Contractual necessity — to provide the features you use (e.g., purchase verification)
  • Legitimate interests — for analytics and app improvement, balanced against your rights
  • Consent — for personalized advertising, obtained via the App Tracking Transparency prompt (iOS) or equivalent

EEA/UK residents have rights under the GDPR / UK GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and the right to object. To exercise these rights, contact support@tap-party.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

By using the Services from outside the US, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States, which may have different data protection rules than your country.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security and encourage you to use the latest version of the App and your device's operating system.
